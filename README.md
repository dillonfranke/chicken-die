## Game Overview

A fast-paced, luck-filled card game of chicken survival! Your goal is to raise and protect your flock of chicks and hens while weathering the chaotic whims of the ‚ÄúChicken Die!" Gather eggs, hatch new chicks, and play action cards to outlast your opponents. Be the last farmer with a chicken standing to win!

**Objective:** Be the last player with at least one chicken (Chick or Hen) in front of you.

## Components

*   **Draw Pile:** A deck of Action Cards.
*   **The Chicken Die:** A 6-sided custom die.
*   **Egg Tokens:** Used to track your egg resources.
*   **Chick Cards:** Represents your basic chickens.
*   **Hen Cards:** Represents your upgraded, egg-laying chickens.
*   **Discard Pile:** Where used Action Cards and "dead" üôÅ chickens go.

## Game Setup

1.  Shuffle the **Draw Pile** and place it in the center of the table.
2.  Create a "Supply" area for the **Egg Tokens**, **Chick Cards**, and **Hen Cards**.
3.  Each player starts the game with:
    *   **3 Chick Cards:** Place these faceup in front of you. This is your "flock."
    *   **5 Cards:** Draw 5 cards from the Draw Pile to form your starting hand.
    *   **1 Egg Token:** A little something to get you started.
4.  The player who most recently ate eggs goes first!

## How to Play (On Your Turn)

On your turn, you must complete the following steps in order:

**Step 1: Collect Eggs**
Collect 1 **Egg Token** from the supply for each **Hen Card** you have in your flock. (Chicks do not produce eggs).

**Step 2: Draw a Card**
Draw the top card from the **Draw Pile** and add it to your hand.

**Step 3: Play Action Cards (Optional)**
You may play any number of Action Cards from your hand. Follow the instructions on each card.

**Step 4: Cash in Eggs (Optional)**
You may spend your **Egg Tokens** to perform the following actions, as many times as you can afford:
*   **Spend 3 Eggs:** Draw 1 card from the **Draw Pile**.
*   **Spend 6 Eggs:** Take 1 **Chick Card** from the supply and add it faceup to your flock.

**Step 5: Roll the "Chicken Die!"**
You **must** roll the 6-sided Chicken Die and immediately resolve its effect.

### The Chicken Die! Outcomes

*   **(2 sides) A Chicken Dies!:** Sad day. You must choose one chicken from your flock (either a Chick or a Hen) and place it in the Discard Pile.
*   **(1 side) Promote!:** Choose one **Chick Card** from your flock and replace it with a **Hen Card** from the supply. If you have no Chicks, this roll has no effect.
*   **(1 side) Roll Again!:** Lucky you? You must immediately roll the die again and resolve the new outcome.
*   **(1 side) Draw a Card!:** Draw the top card from the **Draw Pile**.
*   **(1 side) Collect an Egg!:** Take 1 **Egg Token** from the supply.

## Action Card Types

### 1. Specialty Chicken Cards
*These cards are chickens, but they have special rules. Some can remain hidden in your hand!*
*   **Dino Chicken:** Keep in your hand. Can be revealed and discarded to prevent one of your chickens from being killed by a predator.
*   **Flying Chicken:** Keep in your hand. Can be revealed and discarded to prevent one of your chickens from being stolen.
*   **Mad Scientist Chicken:** Keep in your hand. Can be revealed and discarded to prevent a disease from affecting your chickens.
*   **Robo-Hen:** Place in your flock. Produces 2 eggs instead of 1 during Step 1.
*   **Decoy Chick:** Place in your flock. If an opponent's card would cause one of your chickens to die, you must choose the **Decoy Chick** as the target.

### 2. Protection Cards
*These cards are used to save your flock from harm.*
*   **Immunity:** Play when one of your chickens would die from any source to prevent it.
*   **Chicken Wire:** Play to cancel a predator card (e.g., ‚ÄúCoyote Attack‚Äù).
*   **Cock Block:** Play when an opponent targets you with an Attack card. It cancels their card and immediately ends their "Play Action Cards" step.

### 3. Attack Cards
*These cards are played on your turn to mess with your opponents.*
*   **Coyote Attack:** Choose an opponent. One of their chickens dies.
*   **Die-Die-Die!:** Choose an opponent. They must immediately roll the Chicken Die 3 times in a row.
*   **Hen Swap:** Swap one of your Hens with an opponent's Hen.
*   **Bird Flu:** Choose an opponent. All of their Chicks (not Hens) die.
*   **Omelette:** Destroy 3 **Egg Tokens** from any one player.
*   **Infertility:** Place on an opponent‚Äôs Hen. That Hen no longer produces eggs during Step 1.

### 4. World Altering Cards
*These cards affect all players, including you!*
*   **Demotion:** All Hens currently in play are replaced with Chicks.
*   **Foster Farms:** All Hens currently in play die.
*   **Drought:** Announce "Drought." Until the start of your next turn, no players collect eggs during Step 1.
*   **Fox on the Loose:** Starting with you and going in turn order, every player must roll the Chicken Die 2 times.

### 5. Personal Growth Cards
*These cards provide a benefit only to you.*
*   **Feeding Frenzy:** All of your Chicks are immediately promoted to Hens.
*   **Incubator:** Spend any number of your **Egg Tokens**. For every 2 Eggs you spent, take 1 Chick from the supply.
*   **Farm to Table:** Collect 3 **Egg Tokens** from the supply.
*   **Resurrection:** Take up to 2 chickens from the Discard Pile and add them to your flock.

### 6. Turn Altering Cards
*These cards manipulate the flow of the game.*
*   **Take it or Leave it:** Play just before you roll the die. After you roll, you may choose to ignore the outcome.
*   **End Your Turn:** Play this at any point during Steps 1-4 to immediately end your turn without rolling the Chicken Die.
*   **Reverse:** The order of play is now reversed. You do not roll the die this turn.

## Winning the Game

When a player loses their last chicken, they are **out of the game**.
Play continues until only one player has chickens left in their flock.

**That player is the WINNER!**
